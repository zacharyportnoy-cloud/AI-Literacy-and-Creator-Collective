What We're Building Together

This is the launch of an ongoing collective — not a one-time learning series. We're building a sustained community aimed at shaping the future of AI in education.

Our goal is to support teachers, staff members, and leaders to become not just literate in AI, but creators — actively shaping how AI tools serve diverse educational contexts.

This is about co-creating the future of AI in education so that it reflects the voices, needs, and creativity of educators everywhere.

Our First Partner: Anthropic's Claude

Important: While we're starting with Claude, the collective is model agnostic. The AI literacy skills and creator mindset you'll develop are applicable across all AI tools and platforms.
